Beyond the Frame

Filmmaker for Future: Wildlife
Director 'Sound of Silence' & series editor
CASE STUDY
Beyond the Frame was a series of films produced for COP 26 by the grassroots movement Filmmakers for Future: Wildlife. The films proposed ways to transform the wildlife filmmaking industry to galvanize change in the face of the climate and ecological crisis. This initiative had a real-world impact on how studios operated abroad, leading to more inclusive production teams and efforts to limit their carbon footprints.
