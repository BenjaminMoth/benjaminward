I’m a UK-based, documentary editor, director, and presenter with a passion for wildlife and human interest stories. With a background in conservation and campaigning, my work often focuses on investigative filmmaking and creating impactful narratives.

I’ve had the pleasure of working with film production teams from around the globe, capturing nature’s incredible spectacles. My projects frequently feature character-driven and people-based wildlife stories, and I collaborate with NGOs and production companies to create videos that inspire change.

I have a particular love for moths, butterflies, and birds. On the technical side, I’m a trainer for DaVinci Resolve Studio and can handle both long-form and short-form documentary editing. I’m also skilled in using the Sony A7S, FS7, FX3, and FX6.
